Privacy Policy

QR Scanner respects your privacy and does not collect or store any personal data.

What We Access:

  • Camera: Used only for scanning QR codes. The camera feed is not recorded or stored.
  • Storage: Accessed to save generated QR code images and to load images from your device for QR code decoding.

What We Do Not Do:

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

This app works completely offline and your data stays on your device.
